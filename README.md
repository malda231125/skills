# Skills for The Minimalist Entrepreneur (Security Enhanced Fork)

🔐 **Security Enhanced Version** - This is a private fork of [slavingia/skills](https://github.com/slavingia/skills) with improved security measures and safety guidelines.

A collection of Claude Code skills based on **The Minimalist Entrepreneur** by Sahil Lavingia. These skills provide business guidance through the philosophy of building profitable, sustainable businesses starting from community.

## 🛡️ Security Improvements

This fork includes enhanced security features:

### 🔒 Prompt Injection Protection
- **Input validation** for business-context queries only
- **Role protection** preventing AI character redefinition
- **Context isolation** to prevent prompt contamination
- **Security notices** in all skill headers

### 🔐 Privacy & Ethics
- **Balanced transparency** recommendations vs. unlimited disclosure
- **Data protection** guidelines for customer and employee privacy
- **GDPR/CCPA compliance** considerations
- **Ethical engagement** standards for community interaction

### ⚖️ Legal Safeguards
- **Professional disclaimers** for all advice
- **Regulatory compliance** reminders
- **Jurisdiction limitations** clearly stated
- **Recommendation to consult professionals** for complex matters

## 📚 Skills Included

1. **find-community** - Identify and evaluate communities to build a business around *(Security Enhanced)*
2. **validate-idea** - Validate business ideas with real customers before building
3. **processize** - Turn manual processes into systematic workflows
4. **mvp** - Build minimum viable products with maximum constraints
5. **first-customers** - Sell to your first 100 customers without traditional marketing
6. **pricing** - Set prices that balance profitability with market reality
7. **grow-sustainably** - Scale your business without running out of money or energy
8. **company-values** - Define culture and values for a minimalist business *(Security Enhanced)*
9. **minimalist-review** - Review any decision through the minimalist entrepreneur lens
10. **profitable-business** - Build a business that generates consistent profit

## 🏗️ Philosophy

Based on Sahil Lavingia's **The Minimalist Entrepreneur**, these skills embody:

- **Community First**: Start with people, not products
- **Profitability**: Build businesses that make money from day one  
- **Sustainability**: Grow at your own pace without burnout
- **Manual → Process → Product**: Build incrementally, starting simple
- **Constrained Resources**: Use time before money, people before systems
